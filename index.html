What Is GravesMail?

GravesMail is a standalone desktop email client. You download it, double-click it, and it runs. There is nothing to install. It connects to your existing email account — Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, iCloud, or any IMAP server — and gives you a fast, clean way to read and send email.

The difference between GravesMail and other email apps is what happens behind the scenes. Every message that arrives goes through a five-engine spam and threat detection pipeline before you ever see it. Dangerous messages get caught. Tracking pixels get stripped. And all of this happens on your computer, not on someone else’s server.

Why GravesMail Exists

The big email providers scan your messages, build advertising profiles about you, and let spam and phishing pour into your inbox because filtering it out doesn’t make them money. You are what makes them money.

GravesMail was built because the providers wouldn’t help, law enforcement couldn’t act, and the only real solution was to build technology that makes spam and phishing economically unviable. Key Features

🛡

5-Engine Spam Detection

Bayesian filtering, heuristic analysis, header inspection, domain reputation, and pattern matching work together to catch threats other filters miss.

💻

100% Local

Your email is stored on your computer. Your passwords are encrypted with Windows DPAPI. Nothing is sent to any cloud service unless you explicitly choose to participate in community spam intelligence.

👁

Tracking Pixel Protection

GravesMail detects and strips invisible tracking images that senders use to monitor whether you opened their email, when, and from where.

📦

No Installation Required

Download. Double-click. You’re running. GravesMail is a single executable file with no installer, no registry entries, and no background services.

🧠

Filter That Learns

Every time you mark a message as spam or safe, the filter gets smarter. It starts with 50,000+ pre-trained patterns and builds on them as you use it. Learn more →

📅

Built-in Calendar

Meeting invitations in your email are detected automatically. Accept, decline, or tentatively accept right from the message. Events are stored locally.

📋

Mail Rules

Automate your inbox with rules that move, flag, mark as read, or delete messages based on sender, subject, body content, or attachments.

👥

Contact Manager

Built-in address book with auto-complete in the compose window. Import and export contacts as CSV. No sync to any cloud service.

How It Protects You

When an email arrives, GravesMail runs it through five separate detection engines before it reaches your inbox. Each engine looks at a different aspect of the message: the words in the body, the structure of the headers, the reputation of the sending domain, patterns common to known attacks, and rules you’ve set yourself.

If a message is flagged as spam, it goes to quarantine. If it’s legitimate, it stays. If the filter makes a mistake in either direction, you correct it with one click, and the filter remembers for next time.

Tracking pixels — invisible 1×1 images that senders embed to spy on you — are detected and stripped automatically. You see the message; the sender learns nothing about you.

Community Intelligence

If you choose to opt in, GravesMail can share anonymous spam pattern data — just word scores, never email content — to help improve the filter for all users. It’s off by default and completely optional. Learn how it works →

Getting Started

GravesMail runs on Windows 10 and later. Download the executable, double-click it, sign in with your email account, and choose how you want to sync your existing messages. The app walks you through the entire process, including how to create an App Password if your provider requires one.
