Why I Built This

I built an email client. Not because I wanted to. Because I got tired of waiting for someone else to fix the problem.

My inbox was getting hammered with spam and phishing attacks — some of it straight-up criminal. I contacted the email providers. Nobody responded. I went to law enforcement at every level — local, federal, international — with forensic evidence, names, addresses, everything. Almost nobody acted.

So I built my own solution.

What GravesMail Does

GravesMail is a Windows desktop email client with a 5-engine spam detection system that runs entirely on your machine. No cloud scanning. No data harvesting. No tracking. You choose whether to keep your email on your provider’s server, download a local copy, or pull it all down and remove it from the server entirely.

🧠

Bayesian Filtering

Pre-trained with 57,000+ spam tokens. Learns your specific inbox patterns the more you use it.

🔒

Header Authentication

SPF, DKIM, and DMARC validation catches forged sender addresses that other clients miss.

🔍

Heuristic Analysis

16-pattern engine detects common phishing and spam tactics based on message structure and content.

🌐

Domain Reputation

Validates sending domains against known threat databases to flag suspicious sources.

👁

Tracking Pixel Blocking

Detects and strips invisible tracking images that senders use to spy on whether you opened their email.

💻

100% Local Architecture

You control where your mail lives. Keep it on your provider’s server, sync a local copy, or download everything and remove it from the server. All settings, contacts, and filter data are stored locally in your Windows User Profile.
