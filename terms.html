What GravesMail Is

GravesMail is a desktop email client available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. It connects to your existing email account through standard IMAP and SMTP protocols to download, display, and send email. Your email account and mailbox are provided by your email service provider (such as Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, or iCloud).
